Student Code of Conduct

At Elevate Hub, we are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all learners. By participating in our programs, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.

Students are expected to:

  • Show respect to instructors, peers, and staff at all times.
  • Engage actively and positively in all learning activities.
  • Maintain integrity and avoid plagiarism, cheating, or misrepresentation.
  • Use Elevate Hub resources responsibly and ethically.
  • Refrain from any form of harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior.

Violations of this Code may result in disciplinary action, which could include suspension or removal from the program.

We believe that upholding these standards ensures a supportive and productive learning community for everyone.
